Glitch Art

Spring 2020


All three of these images were constructed using pieces of my paintings that were photographed and glitched. The glitching process illuminates the destruction
of a familiar image and offers fresh material for me to work with in the reconstruction of new compositions in these digital paintings.
As a result, the image is "reborn."
