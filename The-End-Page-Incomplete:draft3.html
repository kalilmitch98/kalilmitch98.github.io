As the vessel is sent into infinite sleep
Iotas of energy ascend their cellular bodies
Passing through the eye of the atmosphere
To look upon their watery womb once more
Time releases its grip on the slippery will
Of the cosmic pulse
The pull of terrestrial energy back to its home in the stars
Rich with love and memory,
The current travels through waves of light
Tumbling in seas of dark matter
Till once again reunited with the Universal Ecology,
And celebrating peace across the galaxy.
