Ancient Nature

My Net Art pages explore a creation story that I authored myself. The story is written more like a poem, and has some differing details depending on how you navigate through the links.
The somewhat non-linear progression with bits and pieces changing depending on what you click on or choose to "listen to" is very similar to way we tell stories verbally from
generation to generation - there can be differing versions.
Cosmologies are generally where I source my imagery from for my paintings and drawings, but this time I decided to write my own story that accompanies my visual pieces.
